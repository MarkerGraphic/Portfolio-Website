Sinisdex
Sinisdex is a variable font which has an axis based on left and right handed calligraphy ductus. 90% of the population of people prefer to use their right hand for writing and working. Therefore, tools and certain technological developments are designed primarily for them. While the 10% of the population prefer to use their left hand, they have to learn to adapt to these biases.
